プレリュード

PRELUDE

•••
Vertizon FRL TKL is an acrylic keyboard kit with a poron gasket mounting system. Vertizon stands for vertical horizon, because it has not only horizontal acrylic layers like traditional stacked acrylic, but also utilizes vertical layers which hide the stacked acrylic looks from the front, back, and sides.

取り付けシステム

MOUNTING SYSTEM

Vertizon is using a poron gasket mounting with a compression to gives user an isolated typing sound experience. It has appropriate amount of flex for comfortable typing experience without being too stiff and bouncy.

キーボードの仕様

KEYBOARD SPECIFICATION

  • FRL TKL or FRL WKL TKL layout
  • Poron gasket mounting
  • 7.5° typing angle
  • Screwless top design
  • Two major parts, top case, and bottom case
  • 20mm front height
  • Milky frosted acrylic color
  • Solderable PCB VIA/QMK compatible

箱の中身

INSIDE THE BOX

  • Vertizon acrylic case
  • Vertizon FR4 plate
  • Dyz FRL TKL PCB - VIA/QMK compatible
  • Poron gasket strips
  • Rubber feet
  • Screws with extras
  • Switch comb
  • Other accessories

キットの組み立て方

COMPLETE THE KIT

To complete your build, you will need:
  • Cherry MX key switches or clones
  • PCB mount screw-in or clip-in stabilizer
  • MX switch compatible keycaps
