PREPARE THE TOOLS

•••
To assembly the kit, you will need:
  • 1. Hex screwdriver
  • 2. Soldering kit
  • 3 .PCB mount stabilizer
  • 4. Cherry MX style switch
  • 5. MX mount keycaps

ステップ 1

STEP 1

Open the case from the bottom by unscrew 12pcs of screws from bottom case.

ステップ 2

STEP 2

Put switch and align it with the plate and the PCB. You can use switch comb to help you put the switch in the correct position. After all switch installed to the plate and PCB, you can solder it. Make sure which one is your preferred layout is between ANSI and WKL before soldering. Test all the switch whether it works properly using key tester such as VIA, VIAL, etc.

ステップ 3

STEP 3

Install Poron gasket on the plate. It’s recommended to put it on the plate rather on the case.

ステップ 4

STEP 4

To complete your build, you will need:
Put the PCB on the case, make sure it sits perfectly before closing the case. Front layer and back layer can be installed by snapping it with both top case and bottom case.

ステップ 5

STEP 5

Screw back the from the bottom case to close the case.

ステップ 6

STEP 6

Install rubber feet on this position

ステップ 7

STEP 7

Your Vertizon FRL TKL is ready to typed on.

ステップ 8

STEP 8

To remap your keyboard from VIA, you will need a JSON file that you can download from here. Make sure to turn on "Show Design Tab" on settings to upload the JSON file.
