プレリュード

PRELUDE

•••
Vertizon HHKB is an acrylic keyboard kit with a hybrid poron gasket and o-ring mounting system. Vertizon stands for vertical horizon, because it has not only horizontal acrylic layers like traditional stacked acrylic, but also utilizes vertical layers which hide the stacked acrylic looks from the front, back, and sides.

取り付けシステム

MOUNTING SYSTEM

Vertizon is using a hybrid poron gasket and o-ring mounting system which supports up to 4 different combinations of a mounting system such as Poron gasket, top poron gasket bottom o-ring, o-ring only, and poron gasket and bottom o-ring. It gives a user the freedom to choose their preference in terms of typing feel and sound profile.

キーボードの仕様

KEYBOARD SPECIFICATION

  • 60% HHKB layout
  • Hybrid Poron and O-ring gasket mounting
  • 7.5° typing angle
  • Screwless top design
  • Two major parts, top case, and bottom case
  • 21mm front height
  • Milky frosted acrylic color
  • PCB VIA/QMK compatible

キットを完成させる

COMPLETE THE KIT

To complete your build, you will need:
  • Cherry MX key switches or clones
  • PCB mount screw-in stabilizer for poron gasket only, clip-in for all mounting compatibility
  • MX switch compatible keycaps
